Why We Built This

Accessories, fashion & books deserve a second chance.

Every year, millions of textbooks become useless the moment exams end. Wardrobes fill up with clothes worn once. Accessories pile up, barely touched. Meanwhile, students spend thousands buying what someone else already has.

YOVO started as a final-year project with a simple question: what if buying and selling used items could be as easy, beautiful, and trustworthy as buying new?

We built the platform we wished existed — a low 12% platform fee, real chat between buyers and sellers, photo verification, and a smooth experience from listing to delivery.

What we stand for

Three principles guide every feature we build.

🌿

Sustainability First

Extending the life of a garment by just 9 months reduces its carbon, water, and waste footprint by 20–30%. Every sale on YOVO is a small act of climate action.

🤝

Community Trust

We keep it peer-to-peer on purpose. Real profiles, real photos, real chat — because trust between two people beats any algorithm.

💰

Low 12% Platform Fee

We charge a transparent 12% platform fee — far below what major marketplaces take. No listing fees, no hidden charges. Sellers keep the majority of every sale, every time.
