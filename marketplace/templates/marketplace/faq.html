❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying and selling on YOVO.

🔍
🛒 Buying
How do I buy an item on YOVO? ▼
Browse items on the homepage, click an item to view details, then click "Add to Cart". Go to your cart and proceed to checkout. Fill in your delivery details and choose a payment method to place your order.
Can I ask questions about an item before buying? ▼
Yes! On any item's detail page, scroll down to the Questions section and type your question. The seller will be notified and can reply. You can also click "Chat with Seller" for a real-time private conversation.
How do I know the item's condition? ▼
Every listing shows a condition badge (New / Like New / Good / Fair / Worn). Sellers are also required to write a description. If a seller has uploaded a bill or receipt, a "Bill Available" badge is shown — click it to view the original purchase proof.
Can I return an item? ▼
YOVO is a peer-to-peer marketplace. Returns depend on your agreement with the individual seller. We strongly recommend chatting with the seller before buying to clarify return policies. Always check the photos and condition rating carefully before purchasing.
📸 Selling
How do I list an item for sale? ▼
Click "Sell" in the navigation bar (you must be logged in). Fill in the title, description, price, category, condition, and upload up to 5 photos. Optionally upload a bill for added credibility. Click "List Item" to publish it instantly.
How many photos can I upload? ▼
You can upload 1 main photo (required) and up to 4 additional photos — 5 photos total. Clear, well-lit photos from multiple angles significantly increase the chance of selling.
Can I edit my listing after posting? ▼
Yes! Go to the item's detail page and click the "Edit Listing" button (only visible to you as the seller). You can update the title, description, price, photos, and condition at any time.
How do I answer buyer questions? ▼
When a buyer posts a question on your item, go to the item's detail page and scroll to the Questions section. Click "Answer" next to any unanswered question. Your answer will be visible to all visitors.
What happens after someone buys my item? ▼
Your item will be marked as Sold automatically. The sale will appear in your Sales & Earnings page (Dashboard → My Sales). You should then contact the buyer via chat to arrange delivery and collect payment directly.

YOVO takes 0% commission — all the money goes directly to you.
💳 Payment
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
YOVO supports three payment methods at checkout:

• 💳 Credit / Debit Card — Visa, Mastercard, RuPay
• 📲 UPI — Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM
• 💵 Cash on Delivery (COD) — Pay when your item arrives
Where does my payment actually go — to YOVO or the seller? ▼
YOVO is a peer-to-peer marketplace, meaning payments go directly between buyers and sellers. YOVO does not hold or process money.

After placing an order, you should chat with the seller to arrange payment transfer (via UPI ID, bank transfer, or cash on delivery). The payment method you select at checkout indicates your preference, but the actual transfer happens between you and the seller directly.

YOVO charges 0% commission — sellers keep 100% of the sale price.
Is my card information safe? ▼
Card details entered at checkout are used only to confirm your payment preference. YOVO does not store card numbers. All transactions are SSL-encrypted. For actual payment transfers, we recommend using UPI for the most secure and instant experience.
Can I get a refund? ▼
Refunds are handled directly between buyer and seller. Since YOVO does not process payments, we cannot issue refunds ourselves. If you have a dispute, use the chat feature to communicate with the seller. We recommend always verifying items through photos and questions before purchasing.
📦 Orders
Where can I see my orders? ▼
Go to Dashboard → My Orders or visit /orders/ directly. Each order shows the status, items, payment method, and a full tracking timeline.
What do the order statuses mean? ▼
• ⏳ Pending — Order received, waiting for seller confirmation
• ✅ Confirmed — Seller confirmed, preparing to ship
• 🚚 Shipped — Item is on the way to you
• 📦 Delivered — Item delivered successfully
• ❌ Cancelled — Order was cancelled
How do I contact the seller about my order? ▼
Open your order from My Orders, then click "Chat with Seller" in the order detail page. This opens a private chat about that specific item.
👤 Account
Is YOVO free to use? ▼
100% free. Creating an account, listing items, buying, chatting — everything is free. YOVO takes absolutely zero commission on any sale.
How do I edit my profile? ▼
Go to Dashboard → Edit Profile or visit /profile/edit/. You can update your name, bio, phone number, city, and profile photo.
Can I delete my account? ▼
Yes. Go to Profile → Edit Profile → Account section and click "Delete Account". This permanently removes your account and all listings. This action cannot be undone.
