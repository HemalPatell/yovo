The real cost of fast fashion & accessories

Facts that should make you think twice before buying new.

👔

Clothes worn only 7-10 times

The average garment in India is worn fewer than 10 times before being discarded. Extending wear to just 30 uses cuts its footprint by over 60%.

📚

Textbooks: one semester, landfill

A university textbook uses 3–4 kg of paper and significant energy to produce, yet most are discarded or abandoned after a single semester.

👜

Accessories: bought once, forgotten fast

Bags, watches, and jewellery are among the most underused items owned. Most accessories are used fewer than 5 times before being shelved or discarded.

🌡

Secondhand = lower carbon

Buying one secondhand item instead of new saves an average of 3 kg of CO₂ — the equivalent of driving a car 12 km.

💸

You save money too

Students who buy and sell on pre-loved platforms save an average of ₹4,000–8,000 per semester compared to buying everything new.
